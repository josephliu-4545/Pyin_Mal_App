# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Fashion enthusiasts** browsing and purchasing clothing online. They want to explore styles confidently, examine product details thoroughly, and make informed purchase decisions. They value a clear view of how clothes look and feel, with options to interact (360° rotation) and assess fit (try-on previews). Context: mobile-first, shopping on the go or leisurely browsing at home.

## Product Purpose

Pyin Mal is a fashion shopping app that connects style-conscious customers with curated clothing collections. The app succeeds when users can easily find, visualize, and purchase clothes they love. Product detail screens are the critical moment: users decide whether to buy based on what they see and learn. The goal is to showcase each item beautifully while providing all necessary information to convert browser to buyer.

## Brand Personality

**Modern, Sleek, Confident**

The brand feels contemporary and premium without being unapproachable. Clean lines, purposeful interactions, and high-quality imagery build trust. The app respects user time: no filler, no noise, just the essentials done exceptionally well.

## Anti-references

- **Cluttered with too many CTAs**: Avoid competing buttons, redundant options, and decision paralysis. Every element should earn its place on the screen.
- **Generic e-commerce templates**: Not Amazon, not eBay, not "standard shopping app." Pyin Mal has a distinct voice.
- **Information overload**: Dense text, small fonts, endless scrolling. Let breathing room and hierarchy guide the eye.

## Design Principles

1. **Product is the hero** — The clothing image dominates visually and mentally. Details and actions support, never compete.
2. **Simplicity with sophistication** — Clean interface powered by advanced interactions (360° rotation, AR try-on). Complexity is invisible; the user experience is effortless.
3. **Trust through clarity** — Minimal hierarchy, honest information, clear next steps. No deception, no dark patterns.
4. **Adaptive experiences** — Light and dark themes support user preference and context (bright daylight vs. evening browsing).
5. **Interactive, not theatrical** — Interactions serve the job (view the clothes, learn about them, buy). No animations for applause.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG 2.1 AA minimum** for all product screens.
- **Color contrast**: Body text ≥4.5:1, labels ≥3:1 on colored backgrounds.
- **Responsive**: Works flawlessly on phones (375px+), tablets (768px+), and larger.
- **Reduced motion**: Every animation has a `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative (crossfade or instant).
- **Color-blind friendly**: Information is not conveyed by color alone (size/shape/label redundancy on color swatches).
- **Touch targets**: Minimum 44x44px for interactive elements.
